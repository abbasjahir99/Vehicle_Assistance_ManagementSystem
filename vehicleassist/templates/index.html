About

Who we are

Vehicle Breakdown Assistance Management System is going to be a good solution for the people who seek help in the remote locations with mechanical issues of their vehicle.

  • Users of the Vehicle Breakdown Assistance Management System will be the registered public
  • They be getting connected with the particular mechanic through the trustworthy Vehicle Breakdown Assistance Management System.
  • User has to register their basic details to get access with this application service.

Administration has to give approval to the registering mechanic after verifying their licensing details for the effective service. Only the licensed mechanics can get listed here while the search. Road assistance for car And there are available mechanic who can come and repair the mechanical issues in the public vehicle.

Vehicle Breakdown Assistance Management System is user front end technology are HTML5, CSS3, JavaScripts and Bootstrap and Backend technology are used Django(Python) & SQLite.

Services

What we do offer

Minor repairs

Vehicle Breakdown Assistance Management System will also provide minor repairing services that can be carried out on the spot to get your car and bike back in working condition.

Flat Tyre

If your car and Bike suffers a flat tyre while you are on the road, the roadside assistance add-on cover will help arrange for a tyre replacement.

Breakdown Support Over the Phone

In the case of minor mechanical errors, you can avail free telephonic car breakdown assistance. You can, thereby, fix the minor problems on your own with help from a mechanic on call.

Spare Keys Assistance

If you happen to lose your keys accidentally or they get stolen, the insurance company will arrange for the pickup and delivery of spare keys to the spot where your car has broken down.

Emergency Fuel

A Vehicle breakdown assistance cover will arrange for fuel delivery to your mentioned location if your car happens to run out of fuel.

Flat Battery

If your car suffers a breakdown due to a flat battery or drained battery, the insurer will make alternative arrangements to repair or replace the battery.
